- Baseline release for GTA Vice City weapon spread control.
- Adds DLL/ASI build targets and a DLL injector helper.
- Forces player and NPC weapon spread paths away from pinpoint accuracy.
- Applies final trace spread for target hits and no-target shots.
- Keeps no-target shot origins stable while spreading the far endpoint.
- Adds INI tuning for accuracy, player spread, final trace spread, minimum close-range spread, no-target trace distance, and logging.
